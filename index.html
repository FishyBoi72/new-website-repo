Different Species of Fish

Tropical Fish

Tropical fish are known for their vibrant colors and are often found in warm, freshwater environments.

Saltwater Fish

Saltwater fish live in the ocean and come in various sizes and shapes, from small clownfish to large sharks.

Freshwater Fish

Freshwater fish inhabit rivers and lakes. Common examples include bass, trout, and catfish.

Fish Habitats

Fish can be found in a variety of habitats, from tropical coral reefs to cold, deep oceans. Each habitat provides unique conditions that influence the fish species living there.
